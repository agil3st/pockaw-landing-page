Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01 January 2025

Welcome to Pockaw! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information You Provide

We do not require registration. However, if you choose to sync your data across devices, minimal personal information like an email address may be collected.

1.2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device (e.g., device model, operating system) to improve app functionality.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data on app usage, such as crash logs, to enhance the user experience.

1.3. Offline-First Approach

Most of your financial data stays on your device. Pockaw does not transmit your financial data unless you choose to sync it with the cloud.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app's features.
  • To offer customer support and troubleshoot issues.
  • To analyze usage patterns for future improvements.
  • To enable secure syncing across devices (if opted in).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. Your data is yours, and we respect your privacy.

We may share anonymized data for analytical purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your financial data is stored securely on your device.
  • For synced accounts, data is encrypted and stored in a secure cloud environment.
  • We employ industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your data.
  • Export your data in a readable format.
  • Delete your data at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

Pockaw may integrate third-party services to enhance functionality, such as cloud syncing or analytics. These services are subject to their own privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Pockaw is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted within the app and are effective immediately upon posting.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: pockaw.app@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy is designed to ensure transparency and protect your data while using Pockaw. By using Pockaw, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
