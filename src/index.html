Why Choose Pockaw?

100% Free & Open Source

No hidden fees, no premium features. Everyone gets full access.

Cross-Platform

Seamless use across Android, iOS*, macOS*, Windows, Linux*, and even the browser.

No Registration Needed

Jump right in without the hassle of creating an account or requesting access like spreadsheet-based does.

No Ads, Always

Say goodbye to annoying ads; Pockaw is focused solely on helping you manage your finances.

Offline-first

Your data is always accessible, even offline. Connect only when you want to sync across devices.

Security Focused

For offline use, you are in charge of your own data. You can also set a Biometrics and PIN if necessary.
