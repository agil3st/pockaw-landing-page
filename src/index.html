Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pockaw really free?

Yes, Pockaw is 100% free and open-source. There are no hidden fees or premium features.

Can I use it offline?

Pockaw is designed with an offline-first approach. You can use it anytime, anywhere.

Is my data secure?

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. Pockaw stores all your data locally on your device, and you have full control over when and how to sync across platforms.
